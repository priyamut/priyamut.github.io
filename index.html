Welcome

Dentist in Phillipsburg, NJ - Jason Birnbaum, DDS, FAGD

Pandemic Update - We are excited to announce that our office is planning to reopen on a limited basis starting May 18th.  

COVID-19 has been a very difficult time for all of us. Many of you have been forced to wait for dental care, and we deeply appreciate your understanding while we kept our office closed to help control the spread of the coronavirus.

Your health and safety are of paramount concern to us.  As we have for the past 35 years, we practice stringent infection control.  We are, however making some changes in the light of this pandemic.

First, if you are experiencing any COVID-19 symptoms or have had exposure to COVID in the past 14 days, we ask that you postpone your dental treatment.  Symptoms of COVID-19 include:

  • Dry cough
  • Fever
  • Shortness of breath
  • Runny nose
  • Sore throat
  • Headache/muscle ache
  • New loss of taste or smell


When you arrive for your appointment, we ask that you call from your car in the parking lot.  We will instruct you if we are ready for your visit.  We do not want people unnecessarily waiting in the reception room. We ask you to wear a mask or facial covering.  We also ask you to make payment arrangements at the beginning of your visit. Our goal is to avoid congregating around Karen and Cathy at the front desk.

The past few months have been a nightmare for the entire world.  Together we will get through this and hopefully, in the not too distant future, 2020 will just be a bad memory.

God Bless

Jason Birnbaum, DDS

At Birnbaum Dentistry in Phillipsburg, NJ,  Dr. Jason Birnbaum and his team provide comprehensive dental care. Our focus is on aesthetically pleasing alternatives to traditional restorative treatment options so you can enjoy a healthier and more beautiful smile.  For over 30 years, we have been providing a wide variety of dental treatments to patients from Phillipsburg and the surrounding communities of Lopatcong, Stewartsville, and Easton.

Personalized Family, Cosmetic & Implant Dentistry

As one of the premier family dentists in Phillipsburg, Dr. Birnbaum provides a comprehensive scope of dental services in our modern, high tech dental office.  Your dental treatment at Birnbaum Dentistry is completed with the utmost care and precision in accordance with your specific dental needs.  As a Fellow of the Academy of General Dentistry, Dr. Birnbaum has committed himself to continued education and stays up-to-date on the latest in modern, innovative dental technology and treatments. Our wide range of family, cosmetic, implant, and laser dental treatment options allow us to conveniently care for patients with all types of dental needs within our office, so our patients don't need to utilize multiple dental specialists like other offices.  Some of our most popular dental services include: 

- One-Visit CEREC Crowns
- Root Canal Treatments
- Porcelain Veneers
- Teeth Whitening 
- Dental Implants
- Dentures
- NightLase Anti-Snoring

As a metal-free dental practice, we focus on our patients’ well-being. Our practice is also equipped with CEREC® CAD/CAM technology that allows us to create custom restorations in one visit. With laser dentistry, we providing composite white fillings without an anesthesia needed (no needle). 

We also provide emergency dental care, so please feel free to contact our office at any time with dental emergencies.   

Patient-Focused Dental Care in Phillipsburg

At Birnbaum Dentistry, we provide dental treatments that meet your oral health and aesthetic goals. In providing both cosmetic and reconstructive dentistry, we utilize porcelain restorations, dental implants, and custom dentures to restore the function and appearance of patients’ smiles. Our accommodating staff ensures your comfort. Dr. Birnbaum has served our Phillipsburg community for 34 years with quality dental care that supports optimal oral health and enhances the beauty of patients’ smiles.

We take the time to know our patients’ goals and concerns, formulating treatment plans that best suit their needs.
